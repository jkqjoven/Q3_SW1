Blue Lock

- SYNOPSIS -

Blue Lock is a series that follows the high school soccer player Yoichi Isagi. A striker for his school team. He decides to pass to a teammate during the last moments of a game instead of taking a shot himself. This ultimately causes his team to lose the game. This made him rethink how he played. He is invited to join a program that aims to make the best striker in the world.

- YOICHI ISAGI -

He is a 2nd year high school student. He previously played as a forward for the Ichinan High School football team. He aims to be the world's best striker.

- MEGURA BACHIRA -

He is a main supporting character of Blue Lock. His 'weapon' dribbling the ball. He is a whimsical forward and is a member of Team Z alongside Yoichi.

- SEISHIRO NAGI -

He is a protagonist in the show. He is one of the skilled players in Blue Lock. He's a naturally talented player with quick reflexes good speed and jumping skills.

- RIN ITOSHI -

He is the younger brother of Sae Itoshi. He has played as a forward on every team he was on. Ever since, he's always been regarded as a top player.

- SAE ITOSHI -

Sae is a prodigy football player who is regarded as the best player in Japan. He is the older brother of Rin Itoshi. Sae is also a member of the Men's World 11.
